# SalesNeuro — Product Documentation & Value Propositions

## What is SalesNeuro?

SalesNeuro is an AI Buyer Psychology Intelligence & Outreach platform. It combines real-time prospect research, behavioral psychology profiling, and AI-generated personalized outreach to help B2B sales teams close deals faster.

---

## Core Problems We Solve

### Problem 1: Generic Outreach Fails
The average B2B cold email response rate is 1-3%. The primary cause is that sellers send the same message to everyone regardless of the buyer's psychology, role, or context.

### Problem 2: Sales Teams Don't Know Their Buyer
Reps spend 5-7 hours researching a single enterprise prospect manually — and still miss behavioral signals that predict how that person buys.

### Problem 3: Messaging is Disconnected from Psychology
Marketing creates generic value propositions. Sales applies them uniformly. Neither adapts to how the specific prospect thinks, makes decisions, or fears risk.

---

## Core Features & Benefits

### Feature 1: Automated Prospect Intelligence
- **What it does**: Pulls real-time data from LinkedIn, news, company filings, and job postings using AI-powered search
- **Benefit**: Eliminates 5-7 hours of manual research per prospect
- **Who cares most**: High-volume SDR teams, AEs managing large territories

### Feature 2: Psychological Profiling Engine
- **What it does**: Analyzes prospect data to generate a Big Five (OCEAN) personality score and DISC behavioral profile
- **Benefit**: Sales teams understand HOW their prospect makes decisions, not just WHAT they do
- **Who cares most**: Enterprise AEs, sales managers coaching reps, revenue operations leaders

### Feature 3: AI Strategist — Benefit Mapping
- **What it does**: Maps the prospect's psychological profile to product features, ranking which value propositions will land hardest
- **Benefit**: Every pitch is tailored to the emotional and rational needs of the specific buyer
- **Who cares most**: AEs running competitive deals, SDRs trying to stand out in crowded inboxes

### Feature 4: Personalized Email Sequence Generation
- **What it does**: Generates a 3-email outreach sequence calibrated to the prospect's communication style and psychological drivers
- **Benefit**: Response rates increase 3-5x versus generic templates
- **Who cares most**: SDRs, BDRs, founders doing outbound, sales managers building playbooks

### Feature 5: CRM-Style Dashboard
- **What it does**: Tracks all profiled prospects, their psychological profiles, and generated email sequences in one place
- **Benefit**: Single source of truth for personalized outreach — no more scattered notes
- **Who cares most**: Sales managers, revenue ops, team leads

## Integration & Technical Details

- **Data Sources**: LinkedIn, Google News, company websites, job boards
- **AI Models**: Anthropic Claude for reasoning and generation
- **Psychology Frameworks**: Big Five (OCEAN), DISC
- **Security**: SOC 2 Type II (roadmap), data never used to train external models
- **Deployment**: Cloud-hosted SaaS, API available for CRM integration
